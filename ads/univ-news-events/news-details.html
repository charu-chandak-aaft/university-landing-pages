AAFT University's Collaboration With New Delhi Film Foundation

AAFT University of Media Arts has announced a significant partnership with the New Delhi Film Foundation. This collaboration aims to enhance AAFT's dedication to excellence in media education, further solidifying its position as a leading institution in India.

Partnership Details

Institutions Involved:

1. AAFT University of Media Arts

2. New Delhi Film Foundation

Objectives of the Collaboration

The primary objective of this partnership is to foster excellence in media education by leveraging the strengths of both institutions. This collaboration will focus on:

1. Enhanced Educational Programs:

AAFT University will offer cutting-edge programs that prepare students for successful careers in film, television, and digital media.

2. Industry-Relevant Skills:

The partnership aims to provide students with skills that are highly relevant to the industry, ensuring they are well-equipped to thrive in the competitive landscape of the entertainment sector.

3. Opportunities for Students:

Students will gain access to unique opportunities and practical experiences that will be instrumental in their professional growth and development.

Significance of the Partnership

This partnership underscores AAFT's commitment to providing top-notch education and industry-relevant skills to its students. By joining hands with the New Delhi Film Foundation, AAFT University of Media Arts aims to create a robust educational framework that nurtures talent and prepares students for the dynamic and evolving entertainment industry.

Conclusion

AAFT University of Media Arts is honoured to partner with the New Delhi Film Foundation. This collaboration is a testament to AAFT's dedication to fostering excellence in media education and providing its students with unparalleled opportunities to succeed in their careers.
