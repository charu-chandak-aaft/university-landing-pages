International Forums

Indo Afghanistan Cultural Forum

The forum was created in August 2016 with renowned music exponent Ustad Eltaaf Hussain Sarahang of Afghanistan nominating Dr. Marwah as the Chair of the Forum. Later Mr. Tahir Qadiry, Charge d Affairs, Embassy of Afghanistan in India accepted the Patron ship of and officially nominated Sandeep Marwah as the Chair the Forum. Vice President of Afghanistan H.E. Abdul Rashid Dostum has also appreciated him for his contribution to the media and entertainment industry. A documentary has been made as a joint venture, cultural week and many other events have been hosted by the forum till date. Alumni of AAft are doing wonders in Afghanistan.

International Forums

Indo Armenia Film And Cultural Forum

H E Dr. Armen Martirosyan Ambassador of Armenia to India inaugurated the Forum in March 2019 and nominated Dr. Marwah to Chair the Forum. A number of Seminars and a webinar have been held in which the Ambassador has participated and shared his words of wisdom. Further planning of a joint venture in films, tourism and other events related to media and entertainment industry are being finalized.

International Forums

Indo Azerbaijan Film And Cultural Forum

Initiated in April 2018 the Forum was launched during the Festival of Films from Azerbaijan by Mr. Jeyhun Aliyev, the then Charge d Affairs of the Embassy of Azerbaijan in India. Later it was no looking back as the Forum was keenly supported by H E Ashraf Shikhaliyev, Ambassador of Azerbaijan to India. Dr. Marwah was invited to Azerbaijan as a State Guest and was also honoured by various organizations of the country.
